Welcome to Campus Critique

Choosing the right university courses and professors is a critical decision for students, yet they often lack reliable, peer-driven insights to guide them. At Campus Critique, we bridge that gap.
Our web-based platform empowers students to share honest feedback on course difficulty, teaching quality, workload, and grading fairness—so you can make informed choices that align with
your learning style and academic goals.
